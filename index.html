Drinks For All

This application is to help find a drink for everyone (of age of course). By putting in your alcohol of choice in the search bar, the application will spit out a list of Drinks that are made with that choice as well as how to make them. Not sure of what to make? Don't worry, just press the randomize button in the bottom right to have our app randomly select a drink for you!
